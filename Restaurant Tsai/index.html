About Us

We serve a wide selection of boba, house-made teas, and Vietnamese coffee. Unlike many boba places, our recipes use fresh fruit and homemade ingredients to bring you a boba experience you are sure not to forget. We are the ONLY place in Thousand Oaks that has FRESH pressed sugarcane juice, something we are proud to introduce to this community (and something that can only be found going into LA). Freshen up every moment and come and visit us! We look forward to serving you!
